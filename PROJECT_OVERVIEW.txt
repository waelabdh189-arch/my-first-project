🎯 PROJECT OVERVIEW

This is a comprehensive educational web application designed to help children
understand, memorize, and master multiplication tables through:

• Visual Learning - Objects, fruits, animals, shapes
• Interactive Exercises - Writing practice, repeated addition
• Educational Concepts - Commutative property, multiplication ÷ division
• Engaging Games - Wheel, Memory, Balloon Pop, Speed Challenge
• Interactive Quizzes - Multiple choice, T/F, fill-blank, timed
• Progress Tracking - Mastery %, achievements, streaks
• Worksheet System - Custom generation with PDF/PNG/JPG export
• Teacher Tools - Worksheet creation and management

LEARNING MODULES (7 sections)

├── Writing Practice ...................... Notebook-style practice sheets

├── Commutative Property .................. 4 × 6 = 6 × 4
├── Multiplication & Division ............. Related fact families
└── Product Matching ...................... Connect equivalent expressions

QUIZZES (4 types)
